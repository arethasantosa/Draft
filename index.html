EDUCATION IN AUSTRALIA

Education contributes to Australia's prosperity and social wellbeing. Education are closely related with employment and workplace relations that support individuals, businesses and nation to prosper.

this tool provides visualisations to examine closely how education in Australia has developed in the past 10 year

Why Study?

There are many reasons people want to study. Based on survey in 2019,
there are 3 main reason people want to engage in education.
The reason are people want to increase job prospects, increase job skills,
personal development and other reason. Almost half want to increase job prospects.
By getting a good job, people can be financially stable and fulfill the basic needs in life.
